Hann’s Farm

I plant small seeds every day—tiny ideas and questions.
I quietly tend them, never chasing a quick harvest.

I simply do today’s work,
steadily, like a farmer.

What matters more than results
is a day lived in my own way.

This is a place to rest for anyone making their own way.
